How It Works

Learn how to become an Eco Hero in 5 simple steps.

1. Identify a Spot

Find a polluted area or waste dump in your vicinity that needs attention.

2. Report & Photo

Upload a "Before" photo with geo-tagging to create a new challenge for the community.

3. Take Action

Clean the area yourself or organize a group cleanup event with your community.

4. Verify Cleanup

Upload an "After" photo. Our AI verifies the location and rewards your effort.

5. Earn & Level Up

Earn points, unlock exclusive badges, and climb the global leaderboard!
